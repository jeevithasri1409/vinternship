title: "A Journey That Started with a Small Hope"
author: Jeevitha Sri
vinternship_id: Jeevithasri1409

Every learning journey begins with a small hope. For me, joining the Vinternship program was also like that. I really wanted to join this internship, and with the support of my friend and the help of Jinal Ma’am, I was able to join.

At the beginning, many things felt confusing. I did not understand everything clearly. But when I started watching the courses on the ViBe platform regularly, the concepts slowly started becoming clearer.

I decided to learn continuously and watch the videos without skipping. Because of that consistency, I was able to understand the topics better and also complete the case studies.

One thing this internship taught me is the importance of consistency. When we keep learning step by step, things that once felt difficult slowly become easier.

Because of my regular learning, I was happy to achieve 2nd place on the ViBe portal. That moment gave me more confidence and motivation to keep improving.

This journey helped me move from confusion to understanding. I am grateful for this opportunity and for everyone who supported me during this internship.
